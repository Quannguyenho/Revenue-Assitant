# RevenueFlow Assistant - Real Demo Video

This guide records the real product. Do not display passwords, OAuth codes, real customer addresses, or confidential transaction IDs.

## Prepare

1. Use a demo Gmail account with one safe payment email.
2. Create a demo Google Sheet and grant the demo Google account edit access.
3. Load the latest RevenueFlow Assistant folder in Chrome and pin the extension.
4. Set Windows display scaling to 100% or 125% and close unrelated tabs and notifications.
5. Connect Gmail and Google Sheets once before recording if the sign-in screen could reveal private information.

## Record

1. Start a 1080p screen recording at 30 FPS.
2. Open Chrome and RevenueFlow Assistant.
3. Show the connected Gmail account without exposing the full address if the video is public.
4. Click the main scan action and wait for the payment inbox to appear.
5. Briefly show the number of matching emails and provider filters.
6. Select one safe transaction.
7. Review the extracted fields and duplicate warning state.
8. Click the Google Sheet save action.
9. Open the configured Google Sheet and highlight the new row.
10. Stop recording after the success state is visible for two seconds.

## Privacy check before publishing

- Remove or blur personal email addresses, transaction IDs, profile IDs, and customer names.
- Confirm no password, OAuth consent code, browser bookmark, or notification appears.
- Use a test transaction rather than a real customer payment whenever possible.
