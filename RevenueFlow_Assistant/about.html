Security and privacy first

RevenueFlow is designed to minimize access, keep people in control, and prevent silent financial actions.

How your data is handled

  • No Gmail password: RevenueFlow never asks for or stores your Google password. Google sign-in and authorization are handled by Chrome and Google OAuth.
  • Read-only Gmail access: Gmail permission is used to find and read payment-related emails. RevenueFlow cannot send, delete, move, or modify email.
  • Controlled Sheet writes: Google Sheets access is used only for the spreadsheet workflow you configure. A write follows a user action or an automation the user explicitly enables.
  • Local preferences: settings and workflow history may be stored in Chrome extension storage on the user's browser profile.
  • No sale of personal data: Netbase JSC does not sell mailbox or payment data collected through RevenueFlow Assistant.

Permissions

GmailRead payment messages after authorization
Google SheetsWrite reviewed records to the selected spreadsheet
Chrome storageRemember settings and local workflow state

Cam kết bảo mật

RevenueFlow không yêu cầu mật khẩu Gmail, không gửi hoặc xóa email, không tự ghi dữ liệu tài chính khi người dùng chưa kiểm tra. Người dùng có thể thu hồi quyền truy cập trong phần bảo mật của tài khoản Google bất kỳ lúc nào.
